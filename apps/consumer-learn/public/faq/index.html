Frequently Asked Questions

What is P3?

P3 is an early-stage protocol that uses reputation scores and social data to help match borrowers and lenders.

Who can use P3?

Availability depends on region, regulation, and partner support. The main app will show if your region is supported during onboarding.

Does checking my P3 score hurt my traditional credit score?

No. Viewing your P3 score does not create a traditional “hard pull” credit inquiry.

Is this financial advice?

No. Nothing on this site or in the product is financial, investment, tax, or legal advice.
