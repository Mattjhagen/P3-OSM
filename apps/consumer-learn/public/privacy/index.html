Privacy & Data Basics

This page is a human-readable overview and does not replace the official legal privacy policy that will ship with the production app.

Core principles

  • Collect the minimum data needed to operate the protocol safely.
  • Explain in plain language why data is used and how it affects your experience.
  • Give you ways to review and, where appropriate, correct information.

Reputation and privacy

The reputation score uses data about your behavior. Some of this comes from actions you take directly in P3; some may come from verified external signals that you choose to connect.

Over time, the main app will publish a full privacy policy and regional disclosures. When that is live, this page will link to the exact legal text.

Questions

If you have questions about how data is handled today, reach out to support@p3lending.space.
