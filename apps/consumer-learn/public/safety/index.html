Safety & Scam Checklist

No protocol can remove risk. This checklist highlights simple habits that reduce avoidable mistakes.

1. Always verify the domain

Make sure you are using the official domains:

  • https://p3lending.space — main app
  • https://learn.p3lending.space — this learning center
  • https://developers.p3lending.space — developer docs

2. Never share secrets

  • Never share wallet seed phrases, private keys, or one-time codes with anyone.
  • P3 staff will never ask for your seed phrase or password.

3. Be skeptical of “too good to be true” offers

Scams often promise guaranteed returns or instant approval if you pay a fee upfront. If something feels off, step away and ask questions.

4. Getting help

If you are unsure whether a message or site is legitimate, contact support@p3lending.space before taking action.
